    DNS

    DNS is the name service provided by the internet for TCP/IP networks.

    • DNS are broken into domains, a logical organization that computer exists in a large network.
    • The domains exists at different levels & connect in a hierarchy that resembles the root structure of atree.
    • Each domain extends from a node above it, begening at the top with the root-level domain.
    • Under those are the top-level domain, under those top are the second-level domains, & on down into subdomains.
    • DNS namspace identifies the structure of domains that combine to form a complete domain.
